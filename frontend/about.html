A Culinary Journey of Passion

Spice Garden was born from a simple dream — to create a place where the rich culinary traditions of India meet the vibrant flavours of the world. Founded in 2015 by Chef Vikram Singh, our restaurant has grown from a small 20-seat eatery to the city's most loved multi-cuisine destination.

Every dish on our menu tells a story — from the smoky tandoor flavours of Punjab to the aromatic biryanis of Hyderabad, from the fiery woks of Sichuan to the delicate pastas of Italy. We believe that great food brings people together.

Our commitment to quality is unwavering. We source fresh ingredients daily from local farms, grind our own spice blends, and prepare every dish with love and precision. That's the Spice Garden promise.
